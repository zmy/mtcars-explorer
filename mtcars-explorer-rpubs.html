Explorer with matcars Data Set

The mtcars data was extracted from the 1974 Motor Trend US magazine, and comprises fuel consumption and 10 aspects of automobile design and performance for 32 automobiles (1973–74 models).

I build a simple shiny application which plot relationships between variables in the data set. Available variables are:

names(mtcars)

 [1] "mpg"  "cyl"  "disp" "hp"   "drat" "wt"   "qsec" "vs"   "am"   "gear"
[11] "carb"

Variable Meanings

Index Name Meaning
1 mpg Miles/(US) gallon
2 cyl Number of cylinders
3 disp Displacement (cu.in.)
4 hp Gross horsepower
5 drat Rear axle ratio
6 wt Weight (lb/1000)
7 qsec ¼ mile time
8 vs V/S
9 am Transmission (0 = automatic, 1 = manual)
10 gear Number of forward gears
11 carb Number of carburetors
